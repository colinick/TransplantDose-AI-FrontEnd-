Welcome to TransplantDose AI

Welcome Message from
A/Prof Terence Kee
Medical Director, Renal Transplant Programme

Welcome to TransplantDose AI — a pilot initiative exploring how artificial intelligence can support precision dosing in renal transplant care.

This platform is currently in its 6-month pilot phase, developed by a multidisciplinary team of transplant clinicians, clinical pharmacologists, data scientists, full stack developers, and epidemiologists. It aims to assist in estimating immunosuppressant doses during the critical first three months post-transplant, using a combination of clinical, lifestyle, and laboratory data.

While TransplantDose AI is not a substitute for clinical judgment, transplant nephrologists may find it a useful adjunct in informing therapeutic decisions. All AI-generated recommendations should be cross-verified with standard therapeutic drug monitoring to ensure accuracy and patient safety.

Your feedback will play a crucial role in improving the accuracy, usability, and clinical relevance of this platform. We look forward to refining and expanding this tool over the coming year — with the hope of launching a full-scale clinical version in the near future.

Thank you for journeying with us as we explore the future of AI-enhanced transplant care.

Purpose of TransplantDose AI

TransplantDose AI is a pilot platform designed to support the transplant team in estimating immunosuppressant doses during the early post-transplant period. By combining clinical, laboratory, and lifestyle data, the tool aims to offer data-driven suggestions that complement clinical judgment—particularly during the high-risk first 90 days post-transplant.
