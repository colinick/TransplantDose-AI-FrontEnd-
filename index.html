How TransplantDose AI Works

From Labs to Personalised Dosing

1) Ingest & unify. We bring together blood tests (C0/C2, creatinine, etc.), genotype, demographics and key clinical metadata.

2) Model & explain. Our patient-specific model estimates the dose needed to stay within the therapeutic window and visualises trends over time.

3) Act & monitor. Clinicians review recommendations with context, then track longitudinal effects in Dosage & Trends. Population risk is surfaced in the Cohort Dashboard.
