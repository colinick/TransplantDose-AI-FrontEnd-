About TransplantDose AI

TransplantDose AI is an innovative tool designed to support renal transplant care by predicting optimal immunosuppressant dosages with precision. By combining advanced AI algorithms with clinical expertise, we aim to help transplant physicians and pharmacists make informed, patient-specific decisions.

Our goal is to enhance treatment accuracy, reduce complications, and improve the long-term health of transplant recipients.
