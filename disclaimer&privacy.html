Disclaimer & Privacy Policy

Disclaimer

TransplantDose AI is currently in a 6-month clinical trial phase as part of ongoing research conducted with the Northshore General Hospital’s research team, transplant nephrologists, and transplant pharmacists. This tool is intended for clinical use only when cross-verified with manual therapeutic drug monitoring (TDM). It is not to be used as a stand-alone decision-making tool for dosing adjustments; all recommendations must be confirmed by qualified healthcare professionals. The system’s purpose is to support healthcare providers in optimising immunosuppressant dosing for renal transplant patients. While the underlying data is assumed to represent real patient scenarios for trial purposes, it has been synthetically generated for development and testing. The platform is subject to continuous improvement and may be updated as new research findings emerge.

By using this platform, you acknowledge and agree to its trial status, the need for manual verification, and its integration as part of a collaborative clinical workflow.

Privacy Policy

Patient-related data within the system should be treated as sensitive clinical information during the trial. All data handling within the system adheres to confidentiality protocols consistent with clinical research standards. No data from this platform will be shared with unauthorised third parties without appropriate approvals.

Additional Notes

This trial is a collaborative effort between the TransplantDose AI development team, the research team, and the clinical transplant care team. Any feedback, dosage adjustments, or unexpected outcomes observed during the trial should be reported to the principal investigator for review.
